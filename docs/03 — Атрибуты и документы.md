## Принцип

> Источник: IMG_6834–6836

The exhaustive list of attributes and documents is given per legal type.

**Legal Entity Types:** Private Limited Company · Public Limited Company · Partnership · LLP (Limited Liability Partnership) · Sole Proprietorship.

> **Note:** For STP processing, customers shall **manually fill** the details outlined below. The rest will be **auto-populated from the Registry**.

---

## Атрибуты, заполняемые клиентом вручную для STP — Entity

| # | Attribute | Data Entry | Section |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | PAN Number | Manual input | Main section of Company's details |
| 2 | Correspondence address (only if different from Registered address) | Manual input | Main section of Company's details |
| 3 | Cheque book required? | Yes/No radio button | Additional Section |
| 4 | Business Industry / Segment | Yes/No radio button | Business Nature Questionnaire |
| 5 | Residency Status of Authorised Signatories | Yes/No radio button | Business Nature Questionnaire |
| 6 | PEP (Politically Exposed Person) | Yes/No radio button | Business Nature Questionnaire |
| — | FATCA | — | Business Nature Questionnaire |

---

## Атрибуты — Individual / Authorized Signatory

| # | Attribute | Data Entry | Section | Details |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Correspondence address | Manual input | VCIP Section | Only if different from Registered address |
| 2 | Authorised Signatory Email ID | Manual input | Board Resolution / Authority Letter / Operating Instructions | |
| 3 | Authorised Signatory Phone Number | Manual input | Board Resolution / Authority Letter / Operating Instructions | |
| 4 | Governance for Authorised signatory changes | Drop Down | Board Resolution / Authority Letter (Operating Instructions) | If the Authorised Signatory is one from Probe42, otherwise ask for PAN number of individual, Name, Designation |
| 6 | PEP | Yes/No radio button | Business Nature Questionnaire | Politically Exposed Person; FATCA |

> Нумерация и часть деталей в источнике обрезаны — `[нечитаемо]` фрагменты сглажены.
