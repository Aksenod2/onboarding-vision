## Business Overview

> Источник: IMG_6819–6820

Current Customer Onboarding process involves manual form filling by the prospect, manual verification of documents by the bank, manual risk assessment, and physical background verification checks. In the current process, successful account opening tasks take around **5 to 7 days**.

**The objective is to reduce the turn around time (TAT) to successful account opening to 1 business day.**

This process covers the onboarding for the below legal entity types and includes auto-population of data for respective attributes and verification of data against registry and screening tools available locally. However, some scenarios require a hybrid approach, based on defined criteria.

## Режимы онбординга (Modes of Onboarding)

> Источник: IMG_6820–6821

The Modes of Onboarding define the different approaches through which customer onboarding is conducted, based on the level of automation, channel of interaction, and extent of verification required. Depending on predefined criteria, onboarding may be processed through **fully digital (STP), Hybrid, or Offline** modes, ensuring appropriate validation, compliance, and due diligence across all scenarios.

| Mode | Description | Key Controls / Features | Condition / Applicability |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Fully Digital (STP)** | End-to-end digital onboarding with auto data population and real-time validation enabling Straight Through Processing (STP). | e-KYC / KYC, registry validation, system-based checks, no manual intervention | Applicable when all predefined STP criteria and validations are successfully met |
| **Hybrid Onboarding** | Partially digital onboarding with system-driven data pre-fill and validations, with human intervention required for exceptions or additional documentation. | Registry validation, partial automation, DVU involvement for exceptions | Applicable when auto-validation fails, data mismatches occur, or additional documents are required |
| **Offline Onboarding** | Fully manual onboarding involving physical document submission and verification. | Physical document verification, manual due diligence by DVU | Applicable for non-STP eligible entity types or where digital/system validation is not feasible |

## Existing Process (as-is)

> Источник: IMG_6821

**Current Onboarding Process:**
- Data collection and validation (KYC verification, sanctions screening) is performed manually across multiple systems, leading to duplication of effort; decisions are conducted in a sequential and partially automated manner.
- Regulatory checks such as KYC verification and sanctions screening are performed manually across multiple systems.
- Most onboarding cases involve multiple rounds of manual review and approval.
- Involvement from CRM team, Ops team and Compliance teams for validation and exception handling.

## Process Description (описание процессов)

> Источник: IMG_6823–6825

| Process | Description | Link |
|---|---|---|
| **CRM** | **Offline Onboarding:** when a customer selects offline onboarding, collect the required documents and forward them to the DVU Manager. **Exception handling** during Onboarding: **Current Account opening not available (CRLC check not success)** — if customer is found ineligible for a current account after verification, present the system-generated reason and provide options (depending on client credit exposure — export/import account and/or collection account) and record customer decision in CRM. | 1D-4: CRM part of F2B Product Opening Process |
| **Self or Assisted Registration** | **Self Registration:** the prospect initiates the onboarding journey through email, authenticates himself and sets up login credentials, then fills out the application form along with required documents and submits for processing. **Assisted Registration:** the prospect initiates the journey through email, authenticates identity and sets up login credentials, then proceeds to complete the KYC, upload required documents, and complete all mandatory validations. | Unified Login - Self/Assisted Registration |
| **CAF** (Current Account Form) | Submission and handling of the Current Account (CA) opening form: completion of the bank-provided form/format, submission of supporting documents, validation of completeness and correctness, handling of exceptions/discrepancies, and tracking from customer submission to internal processing and verification. | OBO |
| **Operating Instructions / Board Resolution / Authority Letter** | A screen to capture Operating Instructions details along with other mandatory details required as part of Board Resolution / Authority Letter, or an option to upload the company's own format. | 1D-8: Operating Instruction Form |
| **Risk Categorization** | Risk categorization based on a risk-nature questionnaire aligned with the type of legal entity. Associated risk factors classify the entity as **Low, Medium or High risk**. | 1D-5: Self-Categorization (Work In Progress) |
| **VCIP (VKYC)** | The Video Customer Identification Process (VCIP) — a digital method of customer due diligence per applicable regulatory guidelines, facilitating real-time remote identity verification, ensuring KYC and AML compliance. **Regardless of legal entity type, if it falls into the High-Risk category / High-Risk Industry list, CRM Head approval is mandatory.** | 1D-7: VCIP (Personal Identification) |
| **DVU Process & Exception Case Handling** | DVU handles alerts/exceptions: continue offline account opening; verify customer's card (autocheck failed); act when CRILC regulatory limit / LEI not fetched; verify VCP visits and VCIP videos; verify third-party BSV report; activate VCIP on request; grant access to Net-Banking and credentials; quality check of existing cases; escalate to Head of DVU and Compliance. | 1D-2: DVU (Data Verification Unit) Process |

> **Дополнительно:** *Onboarding not available* — if the user is not able to open any kind of account for the customer (GFAC fail), the RM should contact the customer and discuss other possible options.

## Пошаговый процесс (Prospect Onboarding Sub-process)

> Источник: IMG_6826–6827. Сквозная таблица шагов (нумерация местами дублируется на фото — сохранена как в источнике).

| Step | Process Stage | Step Description | Action / System Activity | Owner |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **01** | Self Registration | Customer initiates onboarding via online channel | Customer completes self-registration and provides basic details | Customer |
| **01a** | Offline Initiation (CRM) | RM initiates onboarding for offline cases | RM collects documents per checklist and uploads in system | RM / CRM |
| — | Alert to DVU (Offline Flow) | Case routed for verification | System triggers alert to DVU for further processing | System |
| **02** | Initial KYC Check (PAN) | PAN-based validation is performed | System validates PAN and performs initial KYC screening | System |
| — | Alert to CRM | Notification for next steps | System notifies CRM for onboarding progression | System |
| **03** | Operating Instructions | Capture of operating instructions and authorized signatory details | System creates authorized signatory / operating instructions | Customer |
| **04** | Business Nature Assessment | Business-nature questionnaire to determine risk (based on legal entity type and risk factors) | Customer fills questionnaire and provides details for risk assessment | Customer |
| **05** | Auto Data Population | Entity data fetched automatically | System auto-populates legal entity data from registry (PAN/GSTIN) | System |
| **06** | Data Correction | Customer updates incorrect details if correction required | Customer edits data and uploads correction | Customer |
| **07** | Data Confirmation | Prospect confirms filled data on confirmation screen; auto-population from registry | System displays details, customer confirms; if incorrect → 06, else proceed | Customer |
| **08** | VCIP Link Initiation | VCIP link shared with authorized signatory(s) | System sends VCIP link for verification | System / Customer |
| **09** | VCIP Session | Each authorized signatory performs Video KYC | AI-assisted VCIP conducted for identity verification | Customer / System |
| **12** | KYC Auto Checks | Backend verification checks | System performs AML / sanctions / OFAC / KYC validations | System |
| — | Alert to DVU | Exception handling initiation | System routes failed/exception cases to DVU team | System |
| **DVU** | DVU Sub-process | Manual verification and review | DVU reviews documents and resolves discrepancies | DVU Team |
| **10** | CIB Product Opening | Account creation; Net-banking credentials and product setup | System creates login credentials and opens account; checks if BO required | System |
| **11** | CIB Product Activation | Activation upon receipt of initial deposit (and BO completion if required) | System activates account and enables services | System |
| **End** | Completion | Onboarding completed | Customer account is active | System |
